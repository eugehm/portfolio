ML research project tackling the unsolved problem of 2D origami inverse design, developed as part of [Michigan Data Science Team](https://mdst.club/)'s Winter 2026 cycle, supported by compute grants from Google and the UM Great Lakes HPC cluster.

As part of a sub-group of 3, I developed the Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) solution, working on the encoder, node structure, and training pipeline. The model outperformed all benchmarks tested and maintained stable IOU scores even as training data complexity increased progressively across multiple difficulty levels.
