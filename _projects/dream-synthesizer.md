2D platformer with item crafting and Metroidvania-like progression, developed by a team of 6 at [WolverineSoft Studio](https://wolverinesoftstudio.notion.site/).

I designed UI assets (equipment menu, health bar, brick ground) and implemented gameplay systems including armor pickup and equipping, health management (boosts and damage), and enemy behavior (pathfinding, attacking, and death).
