Solo-developed 3D platformer for EECS 298: 3D Technical Art and Animation at the University of Michigan.

I created all 3D assets, designed and applied 3D textures, recorded voice overs, and built the game in Unity on top of a course framework, with custom and modified scripts for additional gameplay behavior.
