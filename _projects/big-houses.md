Housing platform built by a team of 4 at MHacks 2025, designed specifically for University of Michigan students to find housing smarter and faster than the usual chaos of Facebook posts and word-of-mouth leads.

I built the front-end (index, login, and explore pages), designed the logo, contributed to the demo video, and set up the final presentation.
