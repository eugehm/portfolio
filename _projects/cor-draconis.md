City-building RPG developed by a 19-person production team at [WolverineSoft Studio](https://wolverinesoftstudio.notion.site/).

I developed the options menu system, an event-driven architecture managing audio volume, camera/zoom sensitivity, resolution quality, display mode, and dialogue speed, with settings persisting across scenes. Also contributed to the in-game tutorial system alongside two other programmers, and designed particle effects for building assets.
